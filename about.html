IndyPhilo ‚Äî Dare to Think

Independence High School's philosophy club. Non-curricular. Student-led. Open to everyone.

IndyPhilo was founded at Independence High School in Frisco, Texas by students who believe that philosophy isn't just an ancient academic discipline ‚Äî it's the most practical tool a young person can develop.

We are highschoolers interested in the discipline of philosophy. We understand the multi-faceted nature of philosophy. We are wannabe philosophers striving to make philosophy applicable to all.

The Big Three Projects

01

The IndyPhilo Journal

A youth-led opinion and editorial platform. Members write about whatever they want connected to philosophy. Monthly posts are required to maintain membership.

02

The Psych Study

A student-led experiment bridging philosophy and psychology ‚Äî testing philosophical intuitions with real data. Potential for academic publication.

03

Public-Speaking Event

Present your philosophical thinking in front of a real audience. The ability to argue clearly is one of the most transferable skills a student can build.

Partnership with JWAC

Through our partnership with the Junior World Affairs Council, IndyPhilo members gain access to events featuring former government officials, intelligence community professionals, and global policy experts. Members can earn a Global Leadership Certificate and participate in civic service opportunities.
